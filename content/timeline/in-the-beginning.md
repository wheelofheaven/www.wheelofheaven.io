## A Civilization at Its Zenith

The Elohim society had evolved over countless millennia, developing technologies that allowed them to manipulate matter and energy, to traverse the vast distances of space, and most significantly, to understand and control the mechanisms of life. It was during this era that the Elohim turned their attention to the stars. Whether driven by scientific curiosity, the need for new spaces to conduct their experiments, or simply the eternal drive to explore, they began searching the cosmos for suitable worlds.
